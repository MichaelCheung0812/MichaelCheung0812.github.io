Travel to a distant land...

Vynland is a Hytale-based, high fantasy, community driven roleplaying universe in which players assume the role of settlers, adventurers and the like on a newly discovered, untouched continent. Together they will uncover the secrets of this new land while overcoming many challenges, combating both heinous monsters and villainous men. The world of Vynland is governed wholly by players and their decisions.

Players create original characters to interact with the world and others around them, and our seamless systems allow for character advancement without breaking immersion—this ensures every experience is unique!

Welcome to Vynland, traveller. What you do next is up to you.

 
 

A playstyle for everyone

Adventure

Adventure

Explore the untamed wilds of Vynland, delve into the ruins left behind by the ancients and immerse yourself in story-driven world events. Vynland offers a living, breathing campaign-like experience that makes every moment memorable and every encounter unlike any other.

Socialize

Socialize

Whether you decide to traverse the lands with strangers or strike it out alone, Vynland provides plentiful opportunities for interaction with other players, be it on the road or in the vibrant taverns of the land’s grand citadels.

Economy

Economy

Not only warriors may make a name for themselves in Vynland. Artisans are able to leave their own mark on the world by offering utility to dungeoneering parties or contributing to economic systems that mesh seamlessly with roleplay.

Colonize

Politics

Create your own sovereign nations and contend with other factions. Vynland offers a complex, player-driven political environment through Old World interactions that rewards powerplays and nuance in politics.

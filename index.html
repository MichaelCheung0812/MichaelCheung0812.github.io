Travel to a distant land...

The Old World has only just begun to climb out of the depths of a century long war, and remains crippled by poverty and pox. Though many suffer, on the far western horizon an opportunity gleams in the sunlight. The far flung isle of Vynland promises riches and fame to all brave enough to venture there. Is it your destiny to remain, or to strike out and claim a part of this foreign world as your own?

Hytale as a platform

Vynland will be a game server for Hytale, a game by Hypixel Studios. The strong moddability of the game, mechanics and sandbox nature allows for Vynland to be handcrafted and sculpted to needs. The simplicity of voxel based building and model creation will create an immersive and accessible world for all.

On top of this, Vynland will provide tools such as an interactive political mapmode and other tools to facilitate roleplay.
